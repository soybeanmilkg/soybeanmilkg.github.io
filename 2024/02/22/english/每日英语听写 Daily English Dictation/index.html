每日英语听写 Daily English Dictation

B 站课程：每日英语听写 Daily English Dictation 1-400

# Day1

Mother Teresa, who received a Nobel Peace Prize for her work on behalf of the poor, dies in Calcutta India. She was 87 years old.

由于帮助穷人得到诺贝尔和平奖的特丽莎修女在印度加尔各答死去，享年 87 岁。

  • on behalf of 代表
  • Nobel Peace Prize 诺贝尔和平奖
  • Calcutta 加尔各答（印度城市）

# Day2

Jerry, what time do you have? I have 5 o’clock.

Jerry，现在几点了？5 点了。

# Day3

There are three things I’ve learned Never to discuss with people: Religion, Politics and the Great Pumpkin!

在我所学的事物中有三件事不能和他人讨论：宗教，政治和南瓜大王！

  • discuss 讨论
  • religion 宗教
  • politics 政治
  • the Great Pumpkin 南瓜大王（万圣节代表人物，类似于圣诞节中的圣诞老人）

# Day4

Don’t ever let somebody tell you, you can’t do something.

别让别人告诉你，你不能做什么。

# Day5

I have come here to chew bubblegum and kick ass… and I’m all out of bubblegum.

我来到这是为了嚼泡泡糖和收拾人的… 现在我的泡泡糖用完了。

  • chew 咀嚼
  • bubblegum 泡泡糖
  • kick ass （通过语言或暴力）泄愤，发泄不满情绪

# Day6

The secret of life is just live every moment!

生活的秘密就是活在当下！

  • secret 秘密
  • live 活着
  • moment 当下

# Day7

Another rogue trader has cost a bank billions.

又一个流氓交易员让银行损失了数十亿美元。

  • another 又一个
  • rogue 混账的，流氓的
  • trader 交易员

# Day8

German, Rolf Buchholz, is the man with the most piercings; He’s got 453.

德国人，Rolf Buchholz，是穿孔最多的人，他有 453 个穿孔。

  • piercings 穿孔

# Day9

A satellite that was carried to space aboard the shuttle Discovery 20 years ago, is about to fall to Earth, but nobody knows where the debris will hit.

一颗在 20 年前被探险者号带到太空的卫星，即将坠落到地球上，但是没有人知道碎片会击中哪里。

  • satellite 卫星
  • was carried to 被带到
  • shuttle 航天飞机
  • is about to 即将

# Day10

It’s a lofty, probably impossible goal.

一个崇高的，也许不可能的目标。

  • lofty 崇高的
  • goal 目标

# Day11

New York swept Tampa Bay Rays by identical 4-2 scores in a day-night doubleheader Wednesday.

在星期三白天和晚上连续的两场比赛中，纽约队以相同的 4-2 比分横扫了坦帕湾球队。

  • swept 扫
  • Tampa Bay 坦帕湾
  • identical 完全相同的
  • day-night 白天和晚上
  • doubleheader 连赛两场

# Day12

Jim Henson, creator of the Muppets——from Kermit the Frog to Sesame Street——is born in Greenville, Mississippi(MS).

吉姆・亨森出生于密西西比州格林维尔，从青蛙克米特到芝麻街，他是其中布偶的创造者。

  • Muppets 布偶
  • Kermit the Frog 青蛙克米特
  • Sesame Street 芝麻街
  • Greenwich, Mississippi 密西西比州格林维尔

# Day13

Asian tiger mosquitoes are native to the troppics of Southeast Asia, but in the past few years they’ve spread all across the Southeastern United States.

亚洲虎蚊原产于东南亚的热带地区，但在过去的几年里，这种蚊子已经遍布美国东南部。

  • Asian tiger mosquitoes 亚洲虎蚊
  • native to 原产于
  • troppics 热带地区
  • Southeast Asia 东南亚
  • in the past few years 近年来
  • spread 扩散
  • all across 遍及各地

# Day14

Cats reputedly have nine lives, and he clearly wanted to spend at least one of them here in New York City.

据说猫有九条命，他显然想在纽约市消耗其中至少一条命。

  • reputedly 常说
  • clearly 明显的
  • at least 至少

# Day15

The frantic pacing——scene changes every 11 seconds on average——often leaves kids zoned out and spun up; unable to concentrate.

场景平均每 11 秒变化一次，如此疯狂的节奏经常让孩子们精神恍惚，坐立不安；无法集中注意力。

  • frantic 疯狂的，手忙脚乱的
  • pacing 步测；步调
  • often 常常
  • leave sb doing sth 让某人做某事。
  • zoned out 精神恍惚的，分心的
  • spun up 旋转起来
  • concentrate 集中注意力

# Day16

Battered, soaked and flooded; the Philipines is being hit by its 2nd typhoon in a week.

遭受重创、雨水浸泡、洪水肆虐；菲律宾一周内遭遇第二场台风侵袭。

  • battened 挣击的，受伤的
  • soaked 浸湿的
  • flooded 淹没的
  • Philipines 菲律宾
  • typhoon 台风

# Day17

A few years ago, they were thought to be useless——past their prime——not now!

在几年前，它们（流浪狗）被认为没有用处，已经过了最好的时期，但现在不一样了！

  • useless 无用的
  • past their prime 已经过了最好的时期

# Day18

If your are breathing, it is not to late. Get up and get going!

只要还活着，就不算太晚。继续前进吧！

  • get up 起来
  • get going 开动

# Day19

A 67-year-old grandpa has been found alive after stuck for 6 days at the bottom of a ravine.

67 岁的老爷爷在峡谷被困 6 天后，被发现且还活着。

  • stuck for 被困住
  • ravine 峡谷、沟壑、溪谷

# Day20

Saturday, an ultralight plane crashed into a Ferris-wheel at a rural festival in Australia, yet somehow no one the amusement ride nor the plane was hurt.

周六，在澳大利亚的一个乡村节日上，一架超轻型飞机撞上了摩天轮，但不知何故，游乐设施和飞机都没有人受伤。

  • ultralight 超轻型
  • Ferris-wheel 摩天轮
  • rural festival 乡村节日
  • yet somehow 不知何故
  • amusement ride 娱乐设施

# Day21

Heidi won the world over with her forever cute but slightly confused look.

海蒂以她永远可爱但略带困惑的外表赢得了全世界的青睐。

  • won the world over 获得全世界的青睐
  • slightly confused 精神上略显困惑的

# Day22

The project to put them online is expected to be completed by 2016.

将它们（死海卷轴）上传到网上的计划预计将于 2016 年完成。

  • expected to be completed 待完成
